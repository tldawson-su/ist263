About Me

Hello!

I'm Tyler Latimer Dawson and I'm the creator behind Latimer Design.

I'm from Los Angeles, CA and I'm currently studying Advertising at Syracuse University in New York. I've had a passion for both graphic design and soccer for a long time. I've played soccer since I was 5 years old and I started following my local team, the LA Galaxy, in 2014. A few years after that, I started an Instagram account dedicated to following LA Galaxy transfer rumors and news and it was there that I started becoming interested in graphic design, and more specifically sport related designs. I wanted to create better graphics for my own account, so I started teaching myself Photoshop by watching YouTube videos and experimenting with different designs and logos. In 2020, I stopped posting and became too busy with school and sports to keep up to date with news, but I kept designing and experimenting with Photoshop when I had free time.

I returned to my account in 2022, not as a Galaxy news page, but as a graphic design account, and I started posting the old concept kits I had made and working on new designs to post. I named the page Latimer Design after my middle name to keep a little bit of that anonymity but also tie it strongly to myself.

Looking forward, I want to continue to add to my portfolio of kit concepts, delve into other sports designs, and try my hand at redesigning logos.
