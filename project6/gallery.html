Featured below is my portfolio of concept sportswear designs. My style showcases streetwear and art inspired textures, throwbacks, and hypothetical sponsors. After following sports for almost a decade now, I know how deeply communities are tied to their teams. My goal is always to capture the essence of the fanbase, city, or nation with a simple design by combining more modern and simplistic styles with traditional elements and iconography. Enjoy!
